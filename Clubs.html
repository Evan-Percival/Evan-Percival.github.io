UC Riverside 2022-2024

Highlander Combat Robotics

Club Mission:
To enhance students' engineering skills and experience through the design and production of combat robots.
Skills include: CAD, 3D Printing, and RC Circuits

As the president and founding member, I launched this team to expand engineering activities on campus. I chaired meetings, recruited members, produced promotional materials, organized events, raised money, and most importantly acted as a mentor for the members, most of whom had little to no experience. With my experience in building and manufacturing, I helped members improve their designs, ensuring they were practical and efficient. With this success we were able to hold UC Riverside's first ever combat robotics competition on campus.
