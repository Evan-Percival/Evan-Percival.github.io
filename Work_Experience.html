June-September 2023

Orthofix SeaSpine

Manufacturing Engineering Internship

In this intership I gained skills such as:
FDA Documentation, Validation Procedures, Manufactruing Prosses Instructions,
Engineering Presenations, Drafting, Interdisiplanary coolaberation, and Clear Communication

As a manufacturing engineering intern, my main project was to optimize the assembly line by testing and installing new equipment. To accomplish this, I ran multiple force studies on the old and new machines to ensure consistency and accuracy. I also used the new machine to produce engineering samples and analyzed them for potential defects. For documentation and educational purposes, I edited the manufacturing process instructions, wrote technical reports, and trained assemblers on the new equipment. As a side project, I analyzed years of data on material property comparisons and wrote a technical report on my findings to assist future FDA validations.

June-August 2022

Lightning Motorcycles

Mechanical Engineering Internship

In this intership I gained skills such as:
3D scanning and mesh manipulation, CFD through air shapers, FEA through Inventor,
3D printing, Drafting, Hands on fabrication, prototyping, and problem solving

I designed motorcycle parts on Inventor, prototyped them using FDM and SLA 3D printing, and manufactured them using my metalworking skills. For the more important parts, I examined them using Inventor FEA and Air Shapers CFD to ensure practicality and efficiency. To digitize existing parts, I scanned them using an Artec 3D Scanner and then modified those 3D meshes in Blender.
